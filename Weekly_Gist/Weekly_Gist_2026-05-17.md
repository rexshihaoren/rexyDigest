## 🌌 Section 1｜祖先模拟假说的核心论点 / Core Arguments of the Ancestor Simulation Hypothesis
**中文**
- 核心论点：如果一个文明发展到能够创建高保真度的祖先模拟，那么它很可能创建了许多这样的模拟。
- 基于统计概率，我们更有可能生活在模拟中，而不是基础现实中。
- 关键假设：后人类文明将拥有巨大的计算能力来运行复杂模拟。

**English**
- Core Argument: If a civilization reaches a stage where it can create high-fidelity ancestor simulations, it is likely to create many such simulations.
- Based on statistical probability, we are more likely to be living in a simulation than in a base reality.
- Key Assumption: Post-human civilizations will possess immense computational power to run complex simulations.

---
## 🔎 Section 2｜寻找潜在的经验证据 / Seeking Potential Empirical Evidence
**中文**
- 寻找可能“泄露”模拟性质的线索，例如物理定律中的“限制”或“痕迹”。
  - 例: 宇宙常数的精细调整可能指向一个被设计的现实。
  - 例: 模拟中的计算限制或“像素化”效应（例如，普朗克尺度）。
- 考虑模拟中可能存在的“bug”或不一致性。
- 哲学推论：如果模拟者设定了某些参数，这些参数可能会在我们的现实中有所体现。

**English**
- Searching for clues that might "leak" the simulated nature, such as "limitations" or "artifacts" in physical laws.
  - Examples: The fine-tuning of cosmic constants might point to a designed reality.
  - Examples: Computational limits or "pixelation" effects in simulations (e.g., Planck scale).
- Considering potential "bugs" or inconsistencies within the simulation.
- Philosophical inference: If simulators set certain parameters, these might be reflected in our reality.
