**English**
- Traditional LLMs excel at closed-ended tasks via prompt engineering, but lack generalization.
- Truly autonomous agents must adapt to unforeseen circumstances and operate continuously in uncertain environments.
- The shift is from instruction-followers to intent-driven entities that can self-discover and solve problems.

---
## Section 2｜智能体架构的核心组件 / Core Components of Agent Architecture
**中文**
- **规划模块 (Planner)**: 负责长期目标分解、策略制定与行动序列生成。
  - 例: 将“建造房屋”分解为“设计图纸”、“购买材料”、“打地基”等。
- **记忆模块 (Memory)**: 存储经验、世界状态与学习到的模式，支持长期学习与情境理解。
  - 例: 记住过去成功或失败的操作流程，用于指导未来决策。
- **世界模型 (World Model)**: 智能体对环境动态的内部表征，用于预测行动后果。

**English**
- **Planner Module**: Responsible for decomposing long-term goals, strategizing, and generating action sequences.
  - Examples: Breaking down "build a house" into "design blueprints," "purchase materials," "lay foundation."
- **Memory Module**: Stores experiences, world states, and learned patterns to support long-term learning and contextual understanding.
  - Examples: Remembering past successful or failed operation flows to guide future decisions.
- **World Model**: The agent's internal representation of environmental dynamics, used to predict action consequences.
