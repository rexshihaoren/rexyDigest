## ❓ Section 1｜三元悖论 / The Trilemma
**中文**
- 博斯特罗姆提出一个三元悖论：以下三项中，至少一项为真。
  1. 绝大多数像我们这样具备文明等级的物种，在其技术发展到能运行祖先模拟之前就灭绝了。
  2. 任何达到这种技术能力的文明，几乎都不会选择运行大量的祖先模拟。
  3. 我们几乎肯定生活在计算机模拟中。
- 认为这三者之间存在逻辑上的排他性。

**English**
- Bostrom proposes a trilemma: at least one of the following three propositions is true.
  1. The fraction of human-level civilizations that survive to reach a posthuman stage is very close to zero.
  2. The fraction of posthuman civilizations that are interested in running ancestor-simulations is very close to zero.
  3. The fraction of all people with our kind of experiences that are living in a simulation is very close to one.
- Argues that these three are logically exclusive.

- 这些模拟的复杂程度足以产生有意识的体验者，与我们无异。

**English**
- "Posthuman civilization" refers to a civilization whose technological development surpasses current human levels, possessing immense computational power.
- Such a civilization might run a vast number of highly detailed "ancestor simulations" for historical research, entertainment, or other purposes.
- These simulations would be complex enough to produce conscious experiences indistinguishable from our own.

**中文**
- 如果后人类文明运行大量祖先模拟，那么模拟中的“人”的数量将远远超过真实世界中的人数。
- 因此，任何一个随机个体（比如我们自己），更有可能是一个模拟中的个体。

**English**
