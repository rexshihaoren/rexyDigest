English：This paper investigates the emergence and controllability of behaviors in multi-agent systems, proposing a control method combining intervention and feedback.

### 💡 启示 / Implication
中文：理解和控制涌现行为对于设计稳定、可靠的AI系统至关重要，尤其是在自动驾驶、机器人协作等领域。
