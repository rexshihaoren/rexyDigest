**English**  
- The digital physics hypothesis posits that the universe's fundamental nature is information processing, not matter or energy.

- Physical laws can be encoded as computational rules, leading to a predictable, computable reality.

- This approach offers significant advantages in terms of training costs and data dependency, especially in complex or hazardous environments.
